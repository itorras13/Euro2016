Point System

  • Group Stage
    • 1 Point - Guessing the result (win,loss,tie) of each game but not predicting the correct score.
      • Example: You predict Spain wins 2-1 but they win 2-0.
    • 3 Points - Guessing the result and score of each game.
      • Example: You predict France wins 2-1 and they win 2-1.
    • 2 Points - Guessing the team passes but in the wrong position.
      • Example: You predict Portugal passes in first place but they actually pass in second place.
    • 3 Points - Guessing the team passes in the right position.
      • Example: Predicting correctly that Germany passes through as first place in their group.
  • Knockout Stage
    • 4 Points per team - For guessing which eight teams qualify to the quarterfinals
      • Order does not matter for here or future predictions
      • You can choose situations that would not be possible like two teams passing to quarterfinals even though you predicted them to play in round of 16.
    • 6 Points per team - For guessing which four teams qualify to the semifinals
    • 10 Points per team - For guessing which two teams qualify to the finals
    • 6 points - For guessing the third place winner
    • 25 points - For guessing the champion of Euro 2016
    • 10 points - For guessing the top goal scorer of the tournament
  • Tie Breakers
    1. Whoever got the most correct scores in the group stage
    2. Whoever got the most points in the group stage
    3. Whoever predicted the most quarterfinalists correctly
